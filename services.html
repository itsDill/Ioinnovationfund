Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services.

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline for website development varies depending on the complexity of the project. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex e-commerce sites or web applications may take 8-12 weeks or longer. During our consultation, we'll provide you with a specific timeline based on your requirements.

Do you offer website maintenance services?

Yes, we offer website maintenance packages to keep your site running smoothly. Our maintenance services include regular updates, security monitoring, backups, and technical support. Contact us for details about our maintenance plans.

How much does a website cost?

Website costs vary based on your specific requirements, including design complexity, functionality, and content needs. Our custom websites start at $1,499, e-commerce sites at $2,499, and web applications at $4,999. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your project needs.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All our websites are built with responsive design, ensuring they look great and function properly on all devices, including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. Mobile-friendly design is essential for good user experience and SEO performance.

Do you offer hosting services?

Yes, we offer reliable web hosting services that are optimized for performance and security. Our hosting packages include regular backups, security monitoring, and technical support. We can also set up your website on your preferred hosting provider if you already have one.
